Why Zero-Equipment Calisthenics?

At PlexTracker, we believe that relying on expensive gym memberships and machines is a bottleneck to real discipline. Calisthenics—bodyweight training—forces you to master your own biomechanics. By manipulating time under tension, push-up variations, and strict form, you can trigger massive muscle hypertrophy right in your living room.

Our built-in 6-day split is scientifically designed to hit every major muscle group while allowing for adequate recovery. Push days build the chest and triceps, Pull days forge a wide back, and our HIIT protocols strip away stubborn fat to reveal the aesthetic physique underneath.

The 4-Liter Hydration Rule

Muscle isn't just built during the workout; it's built during recovery. Hydration is the most overlooked metric in body recomposition. The PlexTracker dashboard mandates a strict 4L water intake to ensure cellular hydration, flush out lactic acid, and keep your cognitive function sharp for studying and deep work.
